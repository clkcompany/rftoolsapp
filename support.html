Before Contacting Support

For unexpected results, include the tool name, input values, selected units, app version and device model. Do not send sensitive personal information or precise location unless necessary.

Location Permission

GPS Location requires permission while the app is in use. If it was previously denied, enable location access for RF Toolbox in your device settings and try again.

Pro Purchases

A Restore Purchases option is provided on the RF Toolbox Pro screen. Actual store purchasing and restoration will be connected before the paid release.

Support Contact

Email tech@clk.company for product support, technical questions or purchase assistance.
